Presidents (SMC)

Gamma Omicron Chapter · Pi Kappa Alpha

The President of a Pi Kappa Alpha chapter is an esteemed position within our Fraternity. He presides at all chapter meetings, appoints officers and committees, calls special meetings, and is held responsible by the Fraternity for the proper performance of the chapter.

These are the men who were chosen to lead Gamma Omicron since our chartering in 1930.
