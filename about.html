Alumni Association

The Gamma Omicron Alumni Association connects the brotherhood across all generations — from our founding class of 1930 through today's active chapter at Ohio University.

"Once a Pike, Always a Pike."

Your membership in GOAA keeps the chapter resourced, recognized, and rooted in the traditions that make Gamma Omicron one of the most storied chapters in Pi Kappa Alpha.

Get Involved

Your Level of Involvement

We are asking every alumnus to complete the involvement form and select one of two paths. Whether you want to stay connected from a distance or roll up your sleeves and help rebuild — there is a place for you.

Tier One
01
Annual Member

Support the association by paying a modest annual due expected to be around $50. This is for alumni who want to stay connected and contribute financially without taking on leadership responsibilities.

  • Alumni events and networking opportunities
  • Communication platforms and record keeping
  • Long term chapter support initiatives
  • Feed-A-Brother Night supported by alumni
Tier Two
02
Leadership & Foundation Restart Team

Step forward to actively rebuild and lead the Alumni Association and Foundation. This is for alumni who want to take ownership and build something sustainable that will outlast all of us.

  • Establish structure, bylaws, and governance
  • Oversee finances and compliance
  • Drive fundraising and long term strategic planning
  • Coordinate directly with the active chapter leadership
