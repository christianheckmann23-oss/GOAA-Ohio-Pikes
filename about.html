Alumni Association

The Gamma Omicron Alumni Association connects the brotherhood across all generations — from our founding class of 1930 through today's active chapter at Ohio University.

"Once a Pike, Always a Pike."

Your membership in GOAA keeps the chapter resourced, recognized, and rooted in the traditions that make Gamma Omicron one of the most storied chapters in Pi Kappa Alpha.
