    Pi Kappa Alpha Historical Society
    Preserving Our History
    The Pi Kappa Alpha Historical Society has identified three initiatives that will not only help preserve our history, but also make it ready and available for brothers to enjoy. The mission is to compile and create, as best as possible, a holistic history of each and every chapter, make it accessible to every member no matter where they are, and inspire passion for our history along the way!
